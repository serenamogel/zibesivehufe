Shaad Randhawa is a film actor, born in India and exclusively works in the Bollywood film industry. He is the son of Malika and Randhawa and a nephew of the famed actress Mumtaz who is the sister of his mother. While the famed actor Dara Singh is his paternal uncle. He is mainly known for the role played in the Bollywood debut movie named “Who Lamhe” in which he played the role of Nikhil Raj. In the movie, he worked alongside Shiney Ahuja and Kangana Ranaut. While it was directed by Mohit Suri.

Career

He took the start of his career with a Bollywood famed movie “Who Lamhe” featuring Shiney Ahuja and Kangana Ranaut. While it was directed by Mohit Suri. In the movie, he played the role of Nikhil Raj for which he got immense love and praise from fans and a large number of audiences. In 2007, he also worked in the movie “Awarapan” which was another project of Mohit Suri. In the movie, he portrayed the role of Kabir. In 2008, he made an appearance in the movie “Dhoom Dharaka” in which he appeared as a lead actor and undoubtedly won the millions of hearts with his elegant performance. In the movie, Anupam Kher, Jackie Shroff, Gulshan Grover, Razak Khan, and Satish Shah played vital roles, and this way, Shaad Randhawa got the chance to work alongside these big names which is indeed a dream of many aspiring stars. In 2010, he appeared in the movie “Rokkk” in which he appeared as Ranvir. While it was directed by Rajesh Ranshinge. In 2013, he appeared in another rom-com movie and a blockbuster project “Ashiqui 2” in which he appeared as Vivek. In the movie, Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor appeared as lead actors. In 2014, he worked in the movie “Ek Villain” which was another great hit of the year. In the film, he worked alongside other big names including Siddharth Malhotra, Riteish Deshmukh, and Shraddha Kapoor. In 2016, he acted in the movie named “Desh Drohi” which was a comedy movie and other projects are worth sharing to watch.

Achievements

The aspiring actor has worked alongside big stars which is a dream came true for him and still, he hopes to work alongside veteran actors in the future.
