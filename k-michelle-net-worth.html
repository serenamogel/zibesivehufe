Early Life

K. Michelle, whose real name is Kimberly Michelle Pate, was born in 1982 in Memphis, Tennessee. Initially, Michelle intended to pursue a career in law, as she was accepted into several law schools, but decided to follow her passion for music instead.

Career

However, plans for her debut album were scrapped when Jive Records went out of business in 2011. In 2012, Michelle joined the cast of Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta, which showcased her return to the music industry. After the first season, she signed a multi-album deal with Atlantic Records and released her debut album, “Rebellious Soul,” in 2013, which achieved significant success on the charts.

Michelle went on to release several more albums, including “Anybody Wanna Buy a Heart?” in 2014, “More Issues Than Vogue” in 2016, “Kimberly: The People I Used to Know” in 2017, and “All Monsters Are Human” in 2020. She has also embarked on various tours as both an opening act and a headliner.
