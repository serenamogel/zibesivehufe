Hurricane G Husband: Who Was Hurricane G Married To?

Publish date: 2024-02-25

Gloria Rodriguez, widely known as Hurricane G is an American rapper. She was born of Puerto Rican descent according to reports.

Hurricane G was born in 1970 in Brooklyn, New York City in the United States of America.

She made brief appearances on albums by Keith Murray, Redman, Xzibit, Delinquent Habits, Funkdoobiest, Cocoa Brovaz, and others. She was the first female member of the Hit Squad.

Additionally, she was featured in Puff Daddy’s song “P.E. 2000.” All Woman, her long-awaited debut album, was released in 1997.

Hurricane G death

Hurricane G died on 6th November 2022. She died at the age of 52 years.

Hurricane G’s daughter, Lexus Cannon had revealed that her beloved mother died of lung cancer even though it hasn’t been confirmed through her autopsy.

A number of her fans who took to social media stated that the rapper has been battling with ung cancer for years and she was in her termina phase.
