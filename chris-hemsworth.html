One of the most talented, most celebrated Hollywood actors, Chris Hemsworth is an all-time favorite actor. He has built his identity by working on innumerable projects for which he has been getting praised by critics and his fans who can’t stop showering love on him.

Hemsworth, born 11 August 1983, is an Australian actor. Prior to beginning a career in Hollywood, he gained fame playing Kim Hyde in the Australian television series Home and Away (2004-2007). Thor (2011) and most recently in What If…? Hemsworth is best known for his role as Thor in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. In 2021, he became one of the world’s highest-paid actors.

Career

Hemsworth started off his career as an actor in several television shows. Hemsworth appeared in two episodes of the fantasy television series Guinevere Jones as King Arthur in 2002, as well as in one episode of the soap opera series Neighbours and one episode of Marshall Law.

He appeared in an episode of The Saddle Club the following year. Hemsworth auditioned for the role of Robbie Hunter in the Australian soap opera Home and Away in 2004.04. He did not receive the role but was later recalled for the part of Kim Hyde.

Chris Hemsworth has made his career prolific and given many fantastic appearances through he rose to fame. He is mainly known for appearing in ‘The Saddle Club’ in 2003.

Moreover, he has acted in many films, television series, and web series. He has also worked as a voiceover artist. The award-winning actor has lent his voice for ‘Lego Dimensions’ (video game) in 2015.

Achievements

Chris Hemsworth is an award-winning actor and has earned many awards and nominations. Among many, he has won ‘Kids’ Choice Awards for the category of Favorite Male ButtKicker in the ‘Avengers’.
