Nikko Natividad Issue and Scam Who Is His Wife?

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Nikko Natividad famous Filipino actor, model, and dancer got scammed in an investment fund. Here is more on that story.

Nikka Natividad tasted popularity after winning ABS-CBN’S noontime variety show, It’s Showtime. He is also known as a member of #hastags of the same variety show.

Natvidad was also recently on the news for confessing his past abusive behavior towards his wife in an interview with Ogie Diaz.

The variety show star was scammed of 4 million PHP, which is approximately USD 79,120. As per news sources, he fell victim to an investment scam. Natvidad broke his silence of being a victim after a woman was arrested after an entrapment operation.

Nikko Natvidad’s 4 million PHP Scam and Issue – Here’s Detailed Information

Nikko Natvidad, on August 23 revealed that he had been scammed in an investment fund. He revealed in the now-deleted video on Instagram that he was one of the victims of the scammer. The social media star revealed he hadn’t been able to sleep and fell into depression after he lost his hard-earned money.

The now-deleted video featured a news video showing a woman caught after an entrapment operation done by the police. Natvidad expressed he didn’t have any hope for his money to return. He simply hopes the judiciary system will take care of the culprit.

Natvidad also hopes fans will not bash him for being gullible and greedy for not taking calculative decisions for a large sum of money. He appeals that he is speaking only now to help other victims come forward and not be fooled by such easy money schemes.

Although he lost a lot of money, Natvidad looks forward in life and works towards making even more than he once lost. He expressed that perhaps one day, he can laugh at the situation that has just occurred.

Nikko Natvidad’s Wife and Family – An Update on their Current Status

Nikko Natvidad is engaged to be married to Cielo Eusebio. Cielo is also mother of his only son Aiden Natividad.

After years of living together and sharing a son, the couple is finally engaged. The actor was all praise towards his future wife, stating that she has always been there for him without any complaints.

In the recent news, the 27-year-old actor admitted to abusive treatment of his future wife. Stating that there was one time when he raised his hand on his wife. He feels regretful and vows to never hurt her again.

Natvidad explained to interviewer Ogie Diaz sobbing that he feels remorse for being the cause of his wife’s suffering. He adds, she never doubted him and trusts their relationship to the fullest. The actor also vowed to stick to his wife, Cielo, no matter what.
