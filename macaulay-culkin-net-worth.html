Macaulay Culkin | Movies and Television

Actor Macaulay Culkin started working in the entertainment (movies and television) industry at the young age of four. He participated in several small television and film roles.

He even won an American Comedy Award and Young Artist Award for his excellent performance in Home Alone. Similarly, he was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor.

The actor was paid $4.5 million for the movie’s sequel, a strict raise from his previous $110 thousand paycheck.
