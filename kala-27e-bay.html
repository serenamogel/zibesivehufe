Kala'e Bay (Japanese: カーラエ湾 Kālaʻe Bay) is a location on Melemele Island in Alola. It can be accessed from Seaward Cave.

Geography

Kala'e Bay is a small enclosed area of water on the coast of Melemele Island. In Sun and Moon, it has two small sandy islands connected by shallow water, while in Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, it has one bigger grassy island and two small patches of sand sticking out of the water. In both sets of games, there's also a beach on the bay's southwestern edge. This area is visible from a cliff on Route 3 and is popular amongst sightseers.
