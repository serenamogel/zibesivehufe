Paul Stanley Postponed A Concert Last August

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Paul Stanley and his bandmate Gene Simmons have tested positive for COViD-19. Following the news, the band had to postpone a string of shows and their first major Milwaukee concert, which was scheduled at the American Family Insurance Amphitheater on 5 September. The band members will isolate themselves at home for ten days.

Stanley updated his fans on Twitter on 27 August, writing,

“ I had been sick with flu loke symptoms and was tested repeatedly and was negative. Aa of late this afternoon I tested positive.” Everyone on the tour, both band and crew , are fully vaccinated.”

The band clarified in a statement Thursday. Their tour has a covid safety protocol officer on staff full today, reminding everyone to follow all CFC guidelines.

According to their Facebook post, doctors have indicated the tour should be able to resume on 9 September. All previously purchased tickets are said to be honored for the new dates once announced.

Who Is Paul Stanley Ex-wife?

Paul had many past relationships; one of them is Mary Tyler Moore, an American actress. But later, he also got married to Pamela Bowen in 1992 and had one son, Evan Shane Stanley, born in 1994.

After a few years of marriage, they started to have disagreements, and after 9 years of being together, his wife filed for divorce. In 2001, the couple got divorced. 

Is Paul Stanley Still Married?

Paul Stanley married Erin Sutton on 19 November 2005 at the Ritz-Carlton, Huntington in Pasadena, California, after dating for several years. His wife gave birth to three children. They had their first child, Colin Michael Stanley, in 2006, their middle child Sarah Brianna in 2009, and their other child in 2011, and named her Emily Grace.

Paul is a proud husband, and a partner tweeted about his 14th wedding anniversary by posting a beautiful picture of his wife, Erin Sutton, wearing a white dress.

Why Didn’t Paul Stanley Invite Gene On His Wedding?

Paul Stanley once revealed in an interview why he didn’t invite Gene Simmons to his wedding. He said that Gene had been very vocal about not only diminishing the idea of marriage but really having a very disrespectful and demeaning attitude towards people who got married. Therefore, he shared,

“ So for me , it really came down to you don’t belong at my wedding . It would taint it. It would do something. It would be contradictory and for me that would be offensive. He understood. Lo and behold , he got married too.”
