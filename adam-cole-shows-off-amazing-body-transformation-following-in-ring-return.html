Adam Cole Shows Off Amazing Body Transformation Following In-Ring Return

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Adam Cole first appeared at AEW All Out 2021 where he aligned with his former Bullet Club members The Young Bucks and Kenny Omega. Cole wrestled in a few key matches and storylines over the next few months and also reunited with his Undisputed Era stablemates Kyle O’Reilly and Bobby Fish.

However, Adam Cole suffered a severe concussion during his Fatal 4-Way match at Forbidden Door against Adam Page, Jay White, and Kazuchika Okada. He returned for a brief segment on AEW Rampage in August where he and ReDragon turned on The Young Bucks. Since this segment, Cole was absent from AEW television for the next few months.

After an absence of several months, Cole finally returned to in-ring action where he faced and defeated Daniel Garcia in an incredible match.

Following his in-ring return, Adam Cole took to Twitter to showcase his amazing body transformation and it looks like the former WWE Superstar has been hitting the gym pretty hard during his time off.

“I can’t explain how grateful I am to be able to get back to work…let’s get it,” tweeted Adam Cole.

For the past few weeks, AEW has been teasing a potential feud between Adam Cole and Chris Jericho. This will be a dream feud for the ages given the in-ring skills of both men. Until then stay tuned to Ringside News for similar stories.
