Nipsey Hussle &amp; YG Drop &quot;Last Time That I Checc'd&quot; Video

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Following the video for “Rap Niggas,” Nipsey Hussle has returned with another visual taste of his upcoming Victory Lapalbum. Titled “Last Time That I Checc’d,” the Sergio-directed video features fellow Los Angeles rapper YG, and reps his Crenshaw and Slauson neighborhood to the fullest.

Nipsey, who recently inked a deal with Atlantic Records, also delivered the audio of the undeniable banger, which can be found below.

Victory Lap is expected to arrive on February 16.
