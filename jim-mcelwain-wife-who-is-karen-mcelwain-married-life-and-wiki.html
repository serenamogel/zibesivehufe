Jim McElwain, an American football coach at Central Michigan University, was born in Missoula, Montana, in 1962.

He began his football career as an outstanding quarterback at Sentinel High School, receiving all-state honors. Following his career at Colorado, the two-time SEC Eastern Division champion joined Florida. In 2018, the two-time MAC West Division champion joined Michigan as a wide receivers coach before becoming head coach in 2019.
