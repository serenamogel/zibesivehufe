What kind of nails do you use for Hammerschlagen?

Publish date: 2024-03-03

Answer

A bright common nail (12/16d) is hammered into the board in front of each player, approximately a half inch into the wood. In order to take a single swing at a nail, each player must place the wedge-end of their hammer on the log adjacent to their nail and then take their turn. The swinging action must be smooth and continuous both up and down.
