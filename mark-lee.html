Mark Lee – Biography

Mark Lee was born in Singapore on October 16, 1968. Singaporean-Chinese MediaCorp artist and 20-time Star Awards winner who has starred in and hosted various award-winning TV serials and shows. One of his most recognizable roles was a leading part in Police & Thief as Lee Tok Kong.

He worked with Jack Neo on the film Money No Enough. Mark broke through in 1998 when he began appearing on Comedy Nite. He would win the Star Award for Best Comedy Performer in 1998, 1999, and 2003.
