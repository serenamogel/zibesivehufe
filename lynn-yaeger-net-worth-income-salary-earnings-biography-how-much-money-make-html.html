Lynn Yaeger is a contributing fashion editor for Vogue.com as well as a contributing writer for the magazine Vogue. She was a former fashion writer for The Village Voice, where she worked for more than 30 years until retiring. She changed the name of her column from “Elements of Style” to “Frock Star” in February 2007. A frequent contributor to The New York Times Magazine, Style Magazine, American Vogue, Travel & Leisure, and many antiques and collectibles dealers, Yaeger’s work may be seen in a variety of publications. 

Aside from that, Lynn writes a fashion blog for Full Frontal Fashion, a style website produced in partnership with the Sundance Channel. Her quirky personal style, powdered face, and black, cupid’s-bow lipstick have earned her a cult following. In 2008, Lynn was awarded first prize in the National Society of Newspaper Columnists’ category of comedy writing for newspapers with more than 100,000 readers in the area of humour writing for newspapers with more than 100,000 readers.
