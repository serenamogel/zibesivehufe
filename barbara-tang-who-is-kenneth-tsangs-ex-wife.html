He passed away at the age of 86 but his cause of death has not been disclosed yet.

Kenneth before his sudden demised had married thrice and Barbara Tang happens to be one of them.

Well in this article we will be going deep into her personal life to know more about her.

Barbara Tang: Who is Kenneth Tsang’s ex-wife?

Barbara Tang – a columnist and model was the second wife of Kenneth. She is the mother of the late actor’s only daughter, Musette Tsang Mo-suet.

The couple tied the knot in 1980 but parted ways 10 years into their marriage, in 1990 over reasons undisclosed.

She has kept her life private to extent that, her personal information including her date and place of birth is unknown.
