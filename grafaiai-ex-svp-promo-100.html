Grafaiai (Japanese: タギングルex Taggingru ex) is a Colorless-type Stage 1 Pokémon ex card. It is one of the SVP Black Star Promos.

Release information

This card was created as part of the CoroCoro × Pokémon Card Game Illustration & Move Name Contest, featured in the CoroCoro Comic January 2023 issue. Entrants to the contest could use an included postcard to submit both an illustration of Grafaiai and a name of an attack, which would be used as inspiration for the card. Announced in the CoroCoro Comic July 2023 issue, the winning attack name was Poipopoison, and the winning illustration was recreated in a 3DCG style by aky CG Works.

This card was released as a SVP Black Star Promo and was available in the Grafaiai ex Box from April 5, 2024. A oversized version was also included in the box. In Japan, this card was released as a prize for completing a stamp rally at Coro Tour events from July 23-August 27, 2023. Only junior high school students and younger were eligible to participate in this stamp rally. The card could also be obtained by exchanging a voucher included in the CoroCoro Comic August 2023 issue at CoroCoro BASE stores from July 24, 2023. The Japanese print features the CoroCoro Comic logo in the bottom right corner of the artwork.
